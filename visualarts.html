Visual Arts

The Parke House Academy's Art Room is brimming with color, creativity, and artistic expression while engaging both the academic left brain and creative right brain thinkers. The visual arts program educates students in a variety of genres including drawing, painting, printmaking, and sculpture. Students are encouraged to study the work of master artists such as Picasso, Monet, Van Gogh, Cezanne, and Matisse. Their experiences come to life as they discover details about the life, style and work of each artist through our Art Historyoungs Program. This is a unique, one-of-a-kind, PHA inspired program which engages students through monthly art and music presentations. Our students enjoy our clay studio that includes a professional, electric kiln and a pottery wheel that produces exotic glazes, breathing vessels and sculptures. Students' artwork has been shown in local museums and art galleries and on television stations as well as recognized in prestigious art competitions.
