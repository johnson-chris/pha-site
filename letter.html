Leadership and Administration

Your child’s elementary years are a special time of life. From their first experience in a school setting, to the time they are ready to transition into middle school, The Parke House Academy provides an exceptionally comprehensive education. Our curriculum includes Spanish, Computers, Physical Education, Music, Art, Etiquette, Character Development, Library, and Art History. Beginning in Preschool, each grade level is instructed one grade level ahead in Reading and Language Arts. Our Math, Science and Social Studies programs are accelerated at a developmentally appropriate pace. Field trips, guest speakers, professional authors, children’s theater and special family events further complement our program. We also offer many optional after-school programs and extended-day care.

Our campus consists of modern state-of-the-art buildings, art and music studios, a media center which includes our library and computer lab, gymnasium, playground and picnic areas, all nestled among lovely old trees. The front doors, parking lot and hallways are monitored twenty-four hours a day.

We are proud of our innovative and dynamic faculty members who come to us from across the United States. Along with the administration, they share the common goal of establishing a strong academic curriculum in a nurturing environment where young minds can explore and grow. Our success with this unique learning environment is reflected in our students’ outstanding scores on standardized tests and acceptances to excellent middle and high schools.We invite you to come and visit The Parke House Academy.
